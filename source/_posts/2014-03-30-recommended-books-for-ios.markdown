Here are the lists of books that I find useful during iOS development

**Books**
1. iOS By Tutorials series
2. [Effective Objective C](http://www.effectiveobjectivec.com/)
3. iOS Programming the Big Nerd Ranch guide
4. [Pro Objective C](http://www.apress.com/9781430250500)
5. iOS Recipes
6. Functional Reactive Programming for iOS
7. [Pro Objective C Design Patterns](http://www.apress.com/9781430233305)
8. iOS Drawing Practical UIKit solutions
9. [NSHipster - Obscure Topics In Cocoa and Objective-C](https://gumroad.com/l/nshipster)
10. [The NSHipster Fake Book (Objective-C)](https://gumroad.com/l/the-nshipster-fake-book)
11. [CFHipsterRef: Low-Level Programming on iOS & Mac OS X](https://gumroad.com/l/cfhipsterref)
12. Programming iOS 7
13. [iOS Core Animation Advanced Techniques](http://www.informit.com/store/ios-core-animation-advanced-techniques-9780133440751)
